Awards and honours


  • Kalam received 7 honorary doctorates from 40 universities.
  • The Government of India honoured him with " Padma Bhushan " in 1981.
  • He was also honoured with " Padma Vibhushan " in 1990 for his work with ISRO and DRDO.
  • APJ Abdul kalam honoured with highest civilian honour , " The Bharat Ratna " in 2013.
